Mr. Naveen S. Bhardwaj

Naveen S Bhardwaj passed out law from Department of Law, Punjab University, Chandigarh, enrolled with the Bar Council of Punjab and Haryana in the year 2003. Naveen has dealt with Civil matters, Service Law matters, Education matters, Corporate Laws, Criminal matters, Compensation law cases, Company Law matters, Land Revenue Laws, Land Acquisition law matters, Tender Disputes, Government contracts, Consumer Disputes.

Naveen has represented various companies and organizations National Council for Teacher Education, Chaudhary Bansi Lal University, Bhiwani, Indira Gandhi University Meerpur, Rewari, Haryana Power Generation Corporation Limited, Haryana State Federation of Coop Sugar Mills Limited, Panchkula, Haryana Land Reclamation & Development Corporation, Panchkula, Haryana Women Development Corporation, Panchkula, Municipal Corporations of Ambala, Gurugrm, Faridabad, Panipat, Rohtak Central Coop Bank Limited, Rohtak, Panipat Coop Sugar Mills Limited, Panipat, Kaithal Coop Sugar Mills Limited, Kaithal, M3M Limited, Grasim Industries Limited, Haryana State Agricultural Marketing Board, Panchkula.Naveen was appointed as the Law Officer, Government College & Hospital (GMCH), Chandigarh Administration, Chandigarh for the period of August 2005-April 2008.

Passionate about the profession, Naveen engages in-depth and breadth study of the case in hand and leaves no stone unturned, a meticulous researcher, his drafting skills are beyond compare. An advocate through and through, as if the art of advocacy runs through his blood, Naveen’s arguments before the Hon’ble Court are unrivalled.
